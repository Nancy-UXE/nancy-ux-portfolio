Ads Rebranding


Rebranding is the creation of a new look and feel for an established product or company. The usual goal of rebranding is to influence a customer's perception about a product or service or the company overall by revitalizing the brand and making it seem more modern and relevant to the customer's needs.

In Ads platform, rebranding has been a very big effort since we started the multiple journeys of the company moving from brand to brand.

My contribution includes:

  • Worked closely with the sales team
  • Applied Rize Design System
  • Led and applied Theme Centralization
  • Led the Rebranding project multiple times successfully (PM, API, OP, UX, Sales)
What is it? What are the key benefits?

This was a requirement that needed everytime Ads Platform was moved from brand to the next, with the vision of a unified UX for the Verizon Media Ad Platforms. OUR GOAL is to unity our platforms through the power of focused, simple, and crafted designs. Driving toward predictable easy to use experiences, reinforced by the power of the new company brand. As a part of each iteration, we were updating the Ads Platform UI to move closer to the company brand. Our goal is to unify our platforms driving toward predictable easy to use experiences, reinforced by the power of the company brand.

Rebranding Wins:
  • Achived company goal by applying its own brand, to fit with the new strategy.
  • Reduce the development time by 75%
  • Consistency (Color Palette, Icons, Typography)
  • Increase Ads Platform Efficiency and Performance

Ads Platform Theme Centralization
Background

I led restructuring and organizing the SCSS (file containing the Ads Platform CSS, which is responsible for the colors/theme) files across the Ads Platform, which has led to much greater visual/design consistency in the UI.
Over the years Ads Platform got poor structure for SCSS files, that caused a lot of inconsistencies and it made it harder for engineers and designers to collaborate. With the new structure of the SCSS files, we could help the engineers across Ads Platform to be able to work smoothly, with less time and effort to apply the design to different parts of the app. We could reduce the time needed for theming or rebranding the Ads Platform app tremendously.


Results

We finally have a structure and standards that make the UX interaction easier with having a well-defined structure that forces the culture of having a specific visual theme that is followed by all engineers across the board!
Moreover, that was a very important step that needed to apply the RIZE (Design System) look and feel to Ads Platform seemingly with high quality and less inconsistency.
Going forward, as new inconsistencies pop up, there is a clear process for resolving these issues, via the project and process captured in this document. Same applies for any new required rebranding
