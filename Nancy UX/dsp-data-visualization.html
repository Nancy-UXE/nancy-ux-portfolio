Ads Data Visualization


While working on the Prime (Ads Platform revamp) project, we had the privilege to work closely with our users. They gave us important feedback that would improve their experience.

One of those feedbacks is having effective filtering mechanism and data representation in the dashboard that would clearly summarize the performance of their Campaigns, Packages, and/or Lines.

Worked with UXR team and conduct research in different reasons to understand user sentiment, based on different usability metrics, such as ease of use, and Task completion rate.

Pain Points:
Why does Ads Platform UI have Inconsistencies?
  • Ads Platform UI Took over from brightroll
  • Different Ember upgrades
  • Different rebrandings
  • Different teams (development styles - design styles - PM styles)
  • Very big App, many features.
  • Clean ups - Restructuring - Atomic - Grid unit (Different coding styles)
Persona:

Shaping personas iteratively is essential. dividing users into manageable groups and represent each with a typical embodiment! For instance, seeing how Ads Platform app helps these users in their day-to-day activities is very critical for building the constructive experience for users.
Jessi for example looking for saving time and clicks to reach the information, that will get a great impact on the revenue, specially when time is critical and she needs to view information fast and being able to customize it.

Collecting Data:

I have collected the data with the objective of gathering multiple sources of evidence with reference to the problem of this case. I have used different tools in order to collect the relative data, and support the approach and the analysis of the gathered data.
I have looked at Pendo, and checked the heat map, and figured out the frequency that users click in order to reach the information needed. As well, I have done benchmarking along with competitive analysis in order to have better story support this case.

Goals and Guiding Principles:

- Increase users control by creating a data representation for Ads Platform where users feel more comfortable and connected while viewing the campaigns information.

- Increase engagement by providing an effective filtering mechanism for Ads Platform users will give them the ability to view most relevant information they need to see.

User Journey:

Below user journey is the visual representation of the process the user takes to achieve a goal which is in this case the data visualization graphs. Defining that helped to identify different ways to enable the user to achieve their goal as quickly and easily as possible. With that said, user journey maps foster empathy and help product makers put themselves into the shoes of a user. It creates awareness of why users do all the things they do. And it challenges product makers to resist the temptation of building something because it’s feasible, not because it’s needed that way.

Results:

As a result of implementing this case, we could reduce the time needed for users to review the data. Users could take faster decisions and could interact with the information immediately with reducing the number of clicks in less than half. Along with that users could collapse the visual representation in order to be able to view larger amount of the tabular data. For future work, we where thinking to customize the view of the data visual representation, in order to be able to give the users the choice to decide what kind of data to view and interact with.
