Media Search Engine Optimization


Yahoo Media is a group of B2C channels which stands for business-to-consumer, that  refers to the process of selling products and services directly between a business and consumers who are the end-users of its products or services. This meant we had million of users, that can use our channels with or without being logged in.

My contribution includes:

  • I worked on the semantic HTML that was the base for most Yahoo channels at the time.
  • Collaborated closedly with different team members in order to apply the correct SEO rules, and many of the team members referred to me, discuss the best practices.
  • Partnered with product manager in order to manage content workflow based on an understanding of channels priorities
  • Identified and quantified SEO opportunities that would increase overall organic traffic and generate new qualified leads

The importance of SEO for Yahoo Media channels is crucial because it makes our websites more visible, and that means more traffic and more opportunities to convert prospects into customers. In Yahoo Media we were focused on create long-term brand awareness, expand your target audiences and earn more revenue. SEO has major benefits for Yahoo Media and did lead to long-term exponential growth. With investing in SEO, we could build credibility and trust with audiences, making it a pivotal part of a digital marketing strategy.

Below are some of the itmes we took in consideration while apply Yahoo Media SEO:

  • Applied HTML SEO tags (Alt, Title, Headings, Schema markup, etc)
  • Applied Header SEO tags (Title, Content formatting, and headers Meta descriptions, Robots tag)
  • Mobile-friendliness
  • URL structure
  • Adding sitemaps
  • Content strategy
  • Website and page speed (Performance)
  • Social media awareness
